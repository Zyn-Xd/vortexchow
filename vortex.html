Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about the Vortex experience.

How do I place an order on Vortex? +
Download the Vortex app, create an account, browse restaurants near you, add items to your cart, and checkout. Your order will be confirmed instantly.
What cities does Vortex operate in? +
Vortex currently operates in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Ibadan, and Kano. We're rapidly expanding to new cities across Nigeria.
How does live order tracking work? +
Once your order is confirmed, track your rider's location in real-time on the map. You'll receive updates at every stage.
Can I order groceries and pharmacy items? +
Absolutely. Vortex delivers more than food. Order fresh groceries, household essentials, and pharmacy supplies from verified vendors.
